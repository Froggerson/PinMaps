PinMaps: All your travel memories in one place

Do you ever wish you can keep track of your travel memories,
photos, and journal entries all on one map? Well now you can!

PinMaps' Features Include:
  • Inputting multiple pins on a map of where you've been

  • Map can zoom in or out and you can drag to navigate

  • Pins inputted on the map can be dragged for accuracy

  • Form that lets you input details about your trips
    including locations, dates, journal entries, and media links

  • Logging in to save your travel data to your profile
